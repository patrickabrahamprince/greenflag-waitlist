Questions

Before you ask.

What is GreenFlag?
GreenFlag is a weekly invitation-only matchmaking experience in Bangalore, and an upcoming dating app. Instead of swiping, every woman sets a small task and the chat only unlocks once a man actually completes it — effort over swiping.
How much does it cost to attend a date night?
Entry is free for women. Entry for men is ₹599. Every date night is capped at 20 curated guests.
Where are GreenFlag date nights held?
Date nights are held weekly in HSR Layout, Bangalore. The exact venue is shared with guests upon confirmation.
How do I join a date night?
Reserve a seat through the waitlist form below. Seats are limited to 20 guests per week and confirmed on a first-come basis.
Is it really invite-only and curated?
Yes. Every date night is capped at 20 guests, and the GreenFlag app only unlocks a conversation after a task the woman sets is actually completed — no generic openers, no randoms.
How is GreenFlag different from apps like Tinder or Bumble?
Every other app is swipe right, find out it's a red flag later — 500 matches, 3 conversations, 0 follow-through. GreenFlag flips it: she sets the bar, he clears it before a single message gets sent, and there's nothing to say until the task's done.
What happens if he doesn't complete the task in time?
He has 72 hours. If it's not done, the chat never opens — no fallback to a generic message. Flaking has a price tag.
Is GreenFlag available outside Bangalore?
Not yet. GreenFlag currently runs in HSR Layout, Bangalore only, and the app is landing there first before any other city.
